# Sealoft
In Sealoft you play as a newcommer to a quiet and calm seaside town. You work as a mail delivery woman, going around on your brand new Vespa - delivering mail to the townsfolk. Learn to know the inhabitants of Sealoft, and help them get a better day as you run errands to show your support!
